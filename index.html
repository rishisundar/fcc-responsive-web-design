What's Ether and how can I get it?

Ether, denoted as ETH is the cryptocurrency using which transactions are carried out on the Ethereum blockchain. You can get Ether by either mining it or you can buy ETH from exchanges or wallets directly.

Who's Vitalik Buterin and what did he do?

Vitalik Buterin, a Russian-Canadian programmer is one of the co-founders of Ethereum. He proposed Ethereum in 2013. The crowdfunded development of Ethereum started in 2014. On July 30 2015, the Ethereum network went live.
